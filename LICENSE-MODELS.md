## Source datasets

- **HAM10000**: Tschandl, Rosendahl, Kittler. "The HAM10000 dataset, a
  large collection of multi-source dermatoscopic images of common
  pigmented skin lesions." *Scientific Data*, vol. 5, 180161, 2018.
  Licensed under CC BY-NC 4.0.

- **ISIC 2019**: Combalia et al. "BCN20000: Dermoscopic Lesions in the
  Wild." arXiv:1908.02288, 2019. Licensed under CC BY-NC 4.0.
